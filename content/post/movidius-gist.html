Movidius and Intel Neural Compute Stick 2

The new, improved Intel® Neural Compute Stick 2 (Intel® NCS 2) features Intel’s latest high-performance vision processing unit: the Intel® Movidius™ Myriad™ X VPU. With more compute cores and a dedicated hardware accelerator for deep neural network inference, the Intel® NCS 2 delivers up to eight times the performance boost compared to the previous generation Intel® Movidius™ Neural Compute Stick (NCS). — Intel

The sticks allow for deploying AI on the edge, for example on an Raspberry. Version one of the stick is discontinued, and followed by the Neural Compute Stick 2. Currently it can be purchased for around 100 USD.

Intel offers the OpenVino toolkit to transfer models e.g. from Tensorflow (and some other deep learning frameworks) to run on the sticks.
